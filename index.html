UKM Cyber Security ITS (Unit Kegiatan Mahasiswa Cyber Security Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember)
is a student activity unit at the Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember (ITS) in Surabaya, Indonesia,
dedicated to  the field  of  cybersecurity. Stems within the campus community as early as 2019 from
Heroes Cyber Security, this  UKM serves as a  primary hub for students passionate about information
security, ethical hacking, and digital defense.

The unit actively bridges  the gap   between academic  knowledge  and  industry practice, providing
a  platform for students  to develop practical skills through various activities. Members engage in
a range of cybersecurity  disciplines, including web exploitation, binary exploitation, and reverse
engineering. The  UKM  is also  a key  collaborator  in  national and international events, such as
CyberHack  in  2026, a  collaborative   hackathon  with  Xtremax Indonesia  and  AWS Indonesia that
challenged students to apply AI and software engineering to solve real-world industry problems.
